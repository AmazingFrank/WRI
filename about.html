About Us

Focus On The World Economic Forums Future Of Jobs Report

WorldrecordsforImpact will set Guinness world records to draw global attention to social transformation projects across the globe. We believe World record setting can be used as a tool to mobilize large numbers of highly productive people towards a common global goal. Annually a new world record challenge will be announced on this site to mobilize like minds across the globe around a common goal. The inaugural wordrecordforimpact will focus on the World Economic Forums Future of Jobs report.

The World Economic Forumâ€™s Future of Jobs report carried out an extensive research with leading firms across the globe to ascertain the nature and composition of skills needed by the global workforce to navigate around complexities caused by the rise of Artificial Intelligence,robotics,machine learning etc. The list of skills released in the report puts complex problem solving, critical thinking and creativity as the top three.

The Guinness World Records approved our request to set a world record for the largest online business seminar. This online seminar would enlighten participants and draw global attention to the top three skills as outlined in the WEF report.
